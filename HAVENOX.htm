🧒👦👨 Age-Based Protection Levels

🧒

Level 1: Children under 12

  • No device works without parental permission via the app.
  • Customized content with icons & clear colors 🎨.
  • Goal: Complete protection & zero direct access.
👦

Level 2: Teens (12–18)

  • Access only to safe devices (TV, computer).
  • Dangerous devices (heater, stove) auto-blocked 🚫.

Level 3: Adults 18+

❓ Q & A – Common Questions

1️⃣ How is HAVENOX different from existing parental control apps?

🌟 Unlike traditional apps that only limit screen time or internet use, HAVENOX directly controls real electrical devices using microcontrollers and IoT. It combines safety, education, and community awareness in one platform.

2️⃣ What ensures the safety of children if the system fails or loses internet connection?

🔒 Devices are managed by local microcontrollers (Arduino/STM32). Even if the internet is lost, the safety rules remain active, and unauthorized devices stay blocked.

3️⃣ How do you protect user privacy?

🛡️ All data is encrypted and stored in secure cloud servers. Parents have full control over what data is stored or shared.

4️⃣ Why did you choose three age levels?

👶➡️👦➡️👨 Because safety needs change as children grow. Under 12 requires full restriction, teenagers need limited but monitored freedom, and 18+ requires responsibility with awareness.

5️⃣ What is the role of civil organizations in your platform?

🌍 They provide safety reports, accident statistics, and awareness campaigns. Parents can receive local alerts about nearby risks, making HAVENOX not just a family tool, but a community protection system.

6️⃣ Is this system affordable for families?

💰 Yes. The hardware is based on low-cost microcontrollers and sensors, while the software is free or subscription-based at a low cost, making it accessible to most households.
